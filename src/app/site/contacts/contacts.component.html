Outreach

SPEAR is pleased to offer an educational outreach program, featuring information about our rover, equipment and materials used in its operation, and a variety of projects and demos! Students from K-9 will learn about the engineering design process, as well as the many problems and components that have to be solved and implemented to make the rover work!

While we are currently only working with other UAlberta organizations in this capacity, we are working on expanding our program to a wider audience, including schools and similar groups. Stay tuned for more information on this page, or contact us directly!
